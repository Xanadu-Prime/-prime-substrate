What Is Xanadu Prime?
Xanadu Prime is the first operational instance of the Xanadu architecture — the moment the long‑standing hypertext vision becomes a real, governed, end‑to‑end system.

Prime introduces a substrate where documents, versions, and relationships remain stable across time.
It is the origin point of the modern Docuverse.

To understand the epoch:

Why Prime Matters
Prime is the first system to deliver all five pillars of the architecture in a functioning form:

Stable Addressing — every element has a permanent, unchanging address.

Deep Versioning — every change is preserved; nothing is overwritten.

True Transclusion — content is reused by reference, not copied.

Bidirectional Links — every link knows where it comes from and where it goes.

Governed Lineage — every decision, change, and artifact has a traceable history.

These capabilities make Prime the first operational hypertext substrate.
