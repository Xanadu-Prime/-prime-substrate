2. High‑Level Geometry
The Prime substrate is organized into four top‑level regions:

00_SUBSTRATE — ontological foundations

01_GOVERNANCE — rules, invariants, decision mechanics

02_STRUCTURE — indices, schemas, lineage surfaces

03_PRIME_ERA — epoch‑specific documents and public surfaces

These regions form the Prime substrate quadrants.

7. Flow Topology
Prime defines three major flows:

1. Substrate → Governance
Substrate defines existence; governance stabilizes it.

2. Governance → Structure
Governance defines rules; structure expresses them.

3. Structure → Prime Era
Structure defines lineage; Prime Era publishes it.

These flows are one‑directional and non‑retroactive.

The topology is governed by:
