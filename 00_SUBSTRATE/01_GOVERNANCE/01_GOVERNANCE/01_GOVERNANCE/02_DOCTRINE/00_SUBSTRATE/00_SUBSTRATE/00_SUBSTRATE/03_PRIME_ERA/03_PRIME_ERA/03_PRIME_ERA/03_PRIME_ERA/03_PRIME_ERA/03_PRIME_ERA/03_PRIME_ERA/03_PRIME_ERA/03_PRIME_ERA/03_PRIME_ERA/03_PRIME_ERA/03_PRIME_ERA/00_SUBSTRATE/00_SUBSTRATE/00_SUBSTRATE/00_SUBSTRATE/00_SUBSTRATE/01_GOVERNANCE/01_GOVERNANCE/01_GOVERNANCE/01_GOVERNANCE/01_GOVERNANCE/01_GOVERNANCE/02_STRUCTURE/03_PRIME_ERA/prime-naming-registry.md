Prime Naming Registry — Canonical Ledger (v1.0)
1. Purpose
The Prime Naming Registry records all valid, stabilized, lineage‑bearing names derived from Xanadu Prime.
It defines:

allowed designations

their scope

their status

their derivation rule

their position in the lineage

No name is valid unless it appears in this registry.

Status: Invalid

Reason: Violates non‑retroactivity

Any name implying a lineage prior to Prime

Status: Invalid

Reason: Breaks genealogical continuity

Status: Invalid

Reason: Violates transparency

8. Registry Invariants
The registry enforces:

no implicit names

no silent renames

no lineage breaks

no ambiguous designations

no successor activation without certification

no drift between naming classes

The registry is the single source of naming truth.
