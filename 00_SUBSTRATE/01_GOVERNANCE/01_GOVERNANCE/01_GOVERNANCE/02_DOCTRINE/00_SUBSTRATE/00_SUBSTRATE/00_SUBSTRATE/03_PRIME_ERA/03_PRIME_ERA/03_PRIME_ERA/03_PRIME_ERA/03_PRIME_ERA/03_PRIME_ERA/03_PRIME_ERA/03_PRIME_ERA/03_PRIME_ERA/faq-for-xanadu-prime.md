8. How does Prime handle versioning?
Prime uses deep versioning:

nothing is overwritten

every change becomes a new version

every version has a stable address

lineage is preserved across time

This ensures perfect historical fidelity.

9. What is “true transclusion”?
True transclusion means:

content is referenced, not copied

updates propagate automatically

provenance is preserved

links remain stable across versions

This is the core of the Docuverse model.

10. Can Prime interoperate with the web?

Interoperability requires adapters that translate between the two models.
