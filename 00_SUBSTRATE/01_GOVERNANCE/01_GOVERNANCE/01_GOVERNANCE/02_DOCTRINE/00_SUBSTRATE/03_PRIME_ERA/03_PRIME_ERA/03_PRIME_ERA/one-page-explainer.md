What Is Xanadu Prime?
Xanadu Prime is the first operational instance of a governed knowledge architecture built on permanent addressing, deep versioning, and explicit lineage.
It is the root substrate from which all future successors derive.

Prime is not a prototype.
Prime is the origin point.

Modern digital systems drift.

History disappears.

Xanadu Prime demonstrates a different model:

No silent edits

Prime shows how knowledge can be stable, traceable, and governed without becoming rigid.

What Prime Delivers
Prime introduces five architectural pillars:

Permanent Addressing  
Every document and every version has a stable, permanent address.

Deep Versioning  
Nothing is overwritten.
Every change becomes a new, traceable version.

True Transclusion  
Content is referenced without duplication — one source, many views.

Bidirectional Linking  
Every link knows where it came from and where it points.

Governed Evolution  
No drift.
No retroactivity.
No implicit changes.
